The problem we are solving

Environmental degradation is visible everywhere, but often undocumented and unaccountable. Reports disappear into slow, closed systems and communities rarely see who responds or what changes.

What is UniClean?

UniClean is an open-claim environmental action network that combines a social media–style feed with public accountability. Every report has a lifecycle: it starts as a problem and ends as a visible resolution.

It is not a government portal or a one-off campaign. It is a living, global record of environmental problems and the people and institutions who step up to solve them.
